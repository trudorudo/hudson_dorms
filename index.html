About Us

Hudson Dorms was started by two Stevens grads after years of suboptimal living arrangements in Hoboken. From poor maintenance, to mid-semester apartment changes, to lack of functional amenities, it was apparent that there was no "best option" for student housing in Hoboken: Hudson Dorms was created to change that.
We believe students should focus on studying, rather than nervously waiting for an apartment to open up in the hopes of getting off a housing waitlist. How is that even a thing? Every apartment is fully furnished, with the aesthetic managed by a professional interior designer. All amenities are included and we pride ourselves on flexibility to make sure that each group of students has their needs met. We looked at existing choices available in Hoboken and systematically created a better option for each service provided from cheaper housing, to better discounts around town, to a cheaper organic meal plan delivered straight to your door.
